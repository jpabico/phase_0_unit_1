Pair programming is important because it serves as a fail safe. If someone makes a mistake, the other person can catch it. You also learn more by teaching each other.

So what is pairing? Pairing is working together with someone else, ideally at an equal level or expert/novice level. In the latter arrangement, it is beneficial for the expert to drive so the novice gets to explain his or her thoughts.

The driver is the one who is typing out the code. The navigator directs it.
